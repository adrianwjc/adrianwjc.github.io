What is Açaí ?

Açaí (pronounce AH-SIGH-EE) berries (Euterpe oleracea) is a 1cm long, purple-colored fruit grown on the acai palm tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

Rich in antioxidants which fights of free radicals in the body that can damage cells that can lead to disease such as diabetes, cancer and heart disease.

High in Omega fatty acids including Omega 3, 6 and 9. A great source to lower cholestrol and prevent ageing.

Packed with essential nutrients such as fiber, vitamin A, B6, B12, D and minerals Iron, Magnesium, Zinc and Potassium.
